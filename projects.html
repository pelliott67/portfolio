Recordings

I spent many years as a studio musician in Seattle, recording for various CD, radio, and film projects. Here's a little bit about some of the CDs.

The Downtown Mountain Boys - "Heartland" (2014)

Our second recording. This CD was Bluegrass Unlimited's 'feature review' in their August issue.

The Downtown Mountain Boys - "Big Darlin" (2010)

Our first recording, and one of the first projects at StringSound Studio.

Suze Marshall - "Tall Boots" (2005)

An in-depth look at the material of the Sons of the Pioneers featuring some stellar musicians from around the country.

You can hear selections or buy it here.

Catwalk - "Catwalk" (1994)

Original acoustic jazz, with John Miller (guitar), Tom Moran (mandolin), and me.

The CD listed on "Insanelyraremusic.com"

Scott Nygaard - "No Hurry" (1992)

Scott's first recording, on the Rounder Label. A great lineup: Scott (guitar), John Reischman (mandolin), Laurie Lewis (fiddle), John Miller (guitar), Cary Black and Markie Shubb (bass), and me. Scott recorded this right after he moved to the Bay area, before he became editor of Acoustic Guitar magazine.
